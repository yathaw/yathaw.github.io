UX/UI Design

Hello Chircus

www.hellocircus.com

As a UI/UX Designer, I led the redesign of the home wallpaper selling project for Hello Circus, a stylish online wallpaper store. My focus was to redesign the user journey—from product discovery to checkout—creating a clean, modern shopping experience that reflects the brand's aesthetic and helps customers visualize wallpapers in their space with ease.

Hello Circus is an online wallpaper store offering custom and curated wall designs for modern homes. The goal of this project was to revamp the product and shopping experience, making it more intuitive, visually appealing, and conversion-friendly across all devices.

  • Outdated layout and navigation made product discovery difficult.
  • Lack of visual inspiration tools for customers to imagine wallpapers in real spaces.
  • Complex checkout flow caused drop-offs, especially on mobile.
  • Product categories were unclear and difficult to filter.

  • Redesigned product pages with high-res imagery, zoom-in interactions, and contextual room views.
  • Simplified navigation with smart filters by color, theme, and room type.
  • Streamlined the checkout process for mobile responsiveness and fewer steps.
  • Introduced inspirational content like “Shop the Look” and style tips to engage users.

Key Features

  • Responsive and mobile-first product layout
  • Quick-view modal for fast browsing
  • Custom room preview gallery
  • Improved filtering by material, color, and style
  • User-friendly cart and checkout experience
  • Seamless transition between browsing and buying
  • Clean, editorial-style typography and layout
