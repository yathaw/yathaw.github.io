KidoFlix

figma.com/KidoFlix

KidoFlix is is a mobile application designed for children aged 3–12, offering a safe and engaging platform to stream videos while incorporating educational content. The app provides a personalized experience tailored to each child and includes robust parental controls to ensure a balanced approach to entertainment and learning.

KidoFlix is a streaming platform designed exclusively for children, providing safe, age-appropriate content. The app prioritizes education, entertainment, and parental control features to ensure a secure and enjoyable experience.

  • Lack of a safe, child-friendly streaming platform tailored to young users.
  • Parents needed stronger tools to manage what content their children could access.
  • User interfaces were often not intuitive for children aged 3–12 to navigate independently.
  • Balancing entertainment with educational value was challenging.
  • Mobile responsiveness and offline viewing features were missing in many existing solutions.

  • Designed a bright, playful, and child-centric user interface with intuitive navigation.
  • Integrated strong parental control features, including time limits, content filters, and age gating.
  • Developed a content categorization system by age, genre, and educational focus.
  • Implemented responsive design with offline download functionality for greater accessibility.
  • Leveraged AI to deliver personalized content recommendations based on watch history and interests.

Key Features

  • Main Categories: Organized sections like Story, Learning, Genre, Quiz for seamless navigation.
  • Age-Based Content Display: Filters content based on the child's age group (e.g., 3-5 years, 6-8 years, 9-12 years).
  • Streaming Features: Adjustable video settings (resolution options: 480p, 720p, 1080p)., Screen lock to prevent accidental touch interruptions. and Caption/subtitle options for accessibility.
  • Parental Controls: Time limit settings to restrict daily watch time. Block words and content filters for a safe viewing experience.
  • Favorites: Allows kids to save their favorite videos for quick access.
  • Offline Download: Enables downloading of selected videos for offline viewing.
  • Search Functionality: Search by title, category, or keyword to find content easily.
  • Content Library (Channels): Categorized sections featuring different content providers (e.g., Disney, National Geographic Kids).
  • Video Recommendations: AI-based personalized recommendations based on watch history and preferences.
  • Watch History: Allows parents and kids to track previously watched videos.
  • Content Filtering by Age: Parents can set filters to display content suitable for specific age groups.
