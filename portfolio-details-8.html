Brain Mater University

github.com/Brain-Master-University

BrainMaster University is an online learning web application designed to digitalize academic communication and scheduling within universities. The platform streamlines how students and staff interact with schedules, resources, and academic discussions.

The system replaces traditional paper-based processes by offering a centralized platform for managing academic timetables, student engagement, and knowledge sharing. It acts as a digital hub where staff can post updates and class timetables, and students can follow and comment â€” similar to a mini blog system for academic communication.

Universities traditionally rely on physical noticeboards, printed schedules, and informal communication. These methods are inefficient, hard to track, and inaccessible remotely. The challenge was to build a system that digitizes and organizes this academic information while staying user-friendly.

As a Full-stack Developer, I built BrainMaster University using PHP. The platform provides a clear and modern user experience that allows staff to publish class schedules and posts, while students can view and interact in real-time. This not only improves communication but also increases transparency and accessibility for all users.

Key Features

  • Timetable management system for staff and students
  • Blog-style content posting for academic updates
  • Commenting system for student engagement
  • User roles for staff and students
  • Accessible from anywhere with a browser
