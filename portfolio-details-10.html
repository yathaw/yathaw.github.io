Myanmar Play Store is a mobile marketplace app designed to make browsing, discovering, and purchasing digital gadgets and accessories simple and intuitive. The platform offers a wide range of products including vape devices, gaming consoles, software licenses, earphones, backpacks, and cameras, all tailored to the Myanmar market.

The client needed a visually engaging and user-friendly mobile application to replace informal shopping channels. The key challenge was to structure a scalable product catalog, ensure smooth user navigation across diverse categories, and design an interface that balances aesthetics with usability â€” all while keeping performance in mind for low-bandwidth environments.

As the UI/UX Designer, I led the design process in Figma, developing a clean, modern interface with intuitive flows for product browsing, searching, filtering, and checkout. I introduced a category-driven layout with visual emphasis on promotions and new arrivals, while maintaining a consistent and accessible design language throughout the app. The result was a user-centric prototype ready for development, aligning with the client's goals for digital transformation.

Key Features

  • Category-based product browsing
  • Smart search & advanced filters
  • Detailed product pages with images & specs
  • Shopping cart and streamlined checkout
  • Promotional banners for deals & new arrivals
  • Wishlist for saved items
  • Mobile-optimized UI design
  • Figma-based interactive prototype
